You don't have to be confused and suffer silently because there are others like you.


Have you ever felt hurt by someone or felt that something untoward was happening without your permission? This may have been abuse. Abuse that is seldom talked about, acknowledged, or addressed is still abuse and your tears, wounds, and scars are valid. At Journey to mental wealth, we collect interesting, informative, and insightful articles from around the internet about the abuse that people experience but is not always acknowledged. Our goal is to raise awareness about this silent suffering and shed light on ways in which we can help one another journey to a place of mental wealth.
