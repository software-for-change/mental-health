To the people crying unseen tears. I see you.


Abuse that is seldom talked about, acknowledged, or addressed is still abuse and your tears, wounds, and scars are valid. At Journey to mental wealth, we collect interesting, informative, and insightful articles from around the internet about the abuse that people experience but is never acknowledged. Our goal is to raise awareness about this silent suffering and shed light on ways in which we can help one another journey to a place of mental wealth.
